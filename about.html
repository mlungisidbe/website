Who I Am

I'm Mlungisi Dube — an IT Risk and Assurance professional with over 15 years of experience across Ernst & Young, KPMG UK, and NatWest Group. I've worked in Banking, Telecoms, Mining, Manufacturing, Retail and FMCG, and currently lead Technology SOx & Control Testing at NatWest. My work sits at the crossroads of IT governance, controls, audit readiness and AI — and the harder questions about how organisations build resilience in a fast-moving technological world.

I write because thinking in public sharpens thinking in private. The guides and articles here translate 15+ years of hard-won experience into frameworks practitioners can actually use — whether you're a risk manager, an internal auditor, a governance professional, or someone building a career in the field.

Beyond the professional, I'm a reader of history and literature, and an occasional poet. These are not separate pursuits. They are me. The same habits I have bleed over. I question everything, sometimes to the annoyance of those around me, have to be comfortable with ambiguity and look for the truth behind the surface
