About

My Story

I didn't start with a perfectly clear path.

Infact, I did not know that IT audit was a proffession until my third year in University. Like many professionals, I had to figure things out — navigating uncertainty, learning how systems actually work, and understanding where I could create real impact.

Along the way, I realised something important:

The insight

Most people struggle not because they lack ability — but because they don't have the knowledge or the clarity.

Knowledge and Clarity about:

  • How the field works and what skills matter
  • What IT risk and controls are all about
  • How to make an impact in their organisation that matters

That realisation is what shaped how I approach both my work and my writing today.

What I Do

Tech Risk & Assurance — made actionable.

I work in Tech Risk & Assurance, focusing on how organisations manage risk within complex technology environments. This includes:

  • Understanding systems and controls
  • Identifying risks and gaps
  • Strengthening governance and processes
  • SOx compliance and control testing at scale

But beyond the technical side, my work is about something deeper: helping make complexity understandable — and actionable. And now - A new player has entered the fray - Artificial Intelligence
