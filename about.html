Who I Am

I'm Mlungisi Dube — a Risk and Audit professional with a deep interest in emerging technology, particularly artificial intelligence. My work sits at the crossroads of IT governance, controls, and the harder questions about how organisations and societies grapple with the challenges and opportunities presented.

I write because thinking in public sharpens thinking in private. The guides and articles on this site are attempts to translate technical complexity into ideas that practitioners can actually use — whether you're an auditor, a risk manager, a strategist, or simply someone trying to understand the world that AI is building around us..

Beyond the professional, I'm a reader of history and literature, and an occasional poet. These are not separate pursuits. They are me. The same habits I have bleed over. I question everything, sometimes to the annoyance of those around me, have to be comfortable with ambiguity and look for the truth behind the surface
